How It Works

It's as easy as 1, 2, 3!
See below:

Choose your favorite arrangement. We send it on it's way. New selections offered every month.

Ready your blooms for your vase. Stems should be cut at at 45-degree angle, and hydrated right away.

Let your new flowers brighten up your space.
